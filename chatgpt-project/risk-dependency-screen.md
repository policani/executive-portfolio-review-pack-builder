# Risk and Dependency Screen

## Classify signals

- Risk: uncertain future event that may affect objectives
- Issue: current condition already affecting delivery
- Blocker: obstacle preventing progress
- Dependency: reliance on another team, system, vendor, decision, or initiative
- Escalation: item requiring sponsor or executive attention

## Review questions

1. What commitment is threatened?
2. Which initiatives are affected?
3. Is there a named accountable owner?
4. Is mitigation defined?
5. Is sponsor support required?
6. Does the item need sequencing review?

## Output

Create a risk/dependency view that separates facts, assumptions, and recommended executive discussion.
