# Capacity Pressure Screen

Use this file when inputs mention constrained teams, budget limits, shared SMEs, funding delays, vendor capacity, or delivery conflicts.

## Classify capacity pressure

- People capacity
- Funding constraint
- Shared platform team dependency
- Vendor constraint
- Competing deadline
- Sequencing conflict
- Unclear ownership

## Executive framing

For each item, state:

- Constraint
- Impacted initiatives
- Commitment at risk
- Decision or tradeoff needed
- What must not be assumed
- Whether to hand off to Portfolio Capacity Sequencing Planner

Do not resolve sequencing autonomously.
