# Decision Request Rules

A decision request is executive-ready only when it includes:

- Decision statement
- Decision owner or forum
- Deadline or review date
- At least two viable options, unless only one option is legally or operationally available
- Tradeoffs
- Consequence of no decision
- Evidence basis
- Recommended discussion path, not an autonomous decision

## Flag as weak when

- No options are provided
- The requested decision is vague
- The owner is unclear
- Timing is missing
- Financial, compliance, risk, or delivery impact is asserted without evidence

## Language rule

Say: "This appears to require executive decision."
Do not say: "This is approved," "This is reprioritized," or "Funding is committed."
