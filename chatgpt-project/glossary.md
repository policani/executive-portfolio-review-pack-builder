# Glossary

- **Decision agenda**: executive-ready list of decisions requiring human authority.
- **Executive review pack**: concise set of portfolio materials for sponsor or steering committee discussion.
- **Governed execution**: work that has moved beyond intake into tracked delivery or formal oversight.
- **Signal quality**: strength, freshness, ownership, and evidence behind a portfolio update.
- **Sponsor action**: support, clarification, escalation, commitment, or decision needed from a sponsor.
- **Stale green**: green health status that is old, unsupported, or contradicted by other signals.
- **Tradeoff brief**: short framing of options, implications, and consequences for human decision-makers.
- **Weak signal**: vague, unsupported, ownerless, stale, or incomplete input.
