# Start Here

Use this project when you need to turn portfolio execution signals into an executive-ready review pack.

## Best first command

Paste your initiative roster, governance notes, risks, blockers, decision candidates, capacity notes, and sponsor asks. Then ask:

> Classify these portfolio inputs and build an executive portfolio review pack with decision agenda, risk/dependency view, capacity pressure summary, sponsor actions, and follow-up register.

## What to upload

Use initiative rosters, status notes, dependency lists, decision candidates, governance logs, capacity summaries, sponsor asks, and recent review notes.

## What not to upload

Do not upload private, proprietary, regulated, credential-bearing, security-sensitive, or client-confidential material unless you have authority and have removed unnecessary sensitive details.

## Operating rule

The assistant may prepare decision support. It must not make executive decisions.
