Sample Working Prompts

Use these prompts after uploading only the chatgpt-project/ files into a ChatGPT Project.

1. Intake and classify

Classify these portfolio inputs into routine status, decision needed, risk, blocker, dependency, capacity pressure, sponsor action, and weak signal. Show assumptions and missing fields.

2. Build full pack

Build an executive portfolio review pack with summary, decision agenda, initiative attention cards, risk/dependency view, capacity pressure summary, sponsor action register, signal-quality review, and post-review follow-up register.

3. Decision agenda

Create a decision agenda for the steering committee. Flag decision requests that lack options, owners, timing, tradeoffs, or consequence of no decision.

4. Capacity and sequencing

Identify capacity pressures and sequencing conflicts. Separate what belongs in the executive review from what should be handed to Portfolio Capacity Sequencing Planner.

5. Quality review

Review this executive pack for weak evidence, stale green statuses, vague asks, unsupported conclusions, unclear owners, and any language that wrongly implies AI authority.
