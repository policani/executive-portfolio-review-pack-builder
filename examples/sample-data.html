Sample Data

This synthetic scenario represents a technology portfolio with 38 active initiatives, 11 stalled items, 4 primary executive decision requests, 3 funding constraints, and 6 cross-team dependencies.

All names, sponsors, owners, initiatives, constraints, and dates are dummy data for public demonstration. No employer, client, financial-private, security-sensitive, or proprietary information is included.

Files

  • sample-data.csv contains the machine-readable input used by the local tool.
  • sample-output.html is the generated executive review pack.
  • findings-log.csv and findings-log.jsonl contain classified findings.

Scenario themes

  • Data Platform Stabilization creates downstream dependency risk.
  • Customer Portal Modernization and Revenue Forecasting Controls require executive tradeoff review.
  • Several green statuses are stale or weakly evidenced.
  • Stalled items require disposition or owner confirmation.
  • Capacity pressure is concentrated around shared data/platform resources.
